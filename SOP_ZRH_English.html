🔥 Fire / Smoke

  • 1
    Upon discovering fire – attempt to extinguish with nearest extinguisher (CO2 for electrical, Foam for other). If unable to control – step back and evacuate people around you.
  • 2
    Activate manual fire alarm pull station | Alert fire brigade: 118
  • 3
    Floor Warden notifies RSOC: +972 74-723-6000
  • 4
    Evacuate via stairs – no elevator | ERT: last to leave, check all rooms
  • 5
    Assemble at Lagerstrasse | Headcount | Report missing persons
  • 6
    Do not re-enter until explicit clearance from fire brigade or authorized party
Fire Extinguisher types: CO2 → electrical fires (NER / Labs)  |  Foam → all other fires
Fire Marshals: Victor Echem · Elsje Du Preez · Catherine Luke

🚑 Medical Emergency

  • 1
    Call ambulance: 144 | European Emergency: 112
  • 2
    Send someone to retrieve AED immediately:
    3rd floor → near main door behind the OM
    4th floor → Nursing Room
  • 3
    Secure the area – keep bystanders away
  • 4
    Floor Warden notifies RSOC and ensures clear access for rescue services
  • 5
    Do not move the casualty without training
  • 6
    Post someone at building entrance to direct ambulance crew
❤️‍🩹
AED – Automated External Defibrillator
3rd Floor: near main door, behind the OM
4th Floor: Nursing Room | 16 trained first aiders on site

🌍 Earthquake

  • 1
    DROP – Get down on hands and knees immediately
  • 2
    COVER – Take cover under a sturdy desk/table. If none available, cover your head and neck with arms. Move away from windows.
  • 3
    HOLD ON – Stay in position until shaking stops
  • 4
    Once shaking stops – evacuate via stairs to assembly point on Lagerstrasse
  • 5
    Notify RSOC: +972 74-723-6000
  • 6
    Watch for aftershocks | Stay clear of debris and downed power lines outside
  • 7
    Do not use elevator | Do not re-enter without clearance from rescue services

🔫 Security Incident / Violence – Run · Hide · Fight

  • 1
    RUN – Evacuate the area immediately, leave belongings behind
  • 2
    HIDE – If you cannot run: lock/barricade door, turn off lights, stay low and silent
  • 3
    FIGHT – Only as a last resort, use any available means
  • 4
    Floor Warden reports to Police 117 and RSOC +972 74-723-6000. Initiate lockdown.
  • 5
    Do not attempt solo rescue – wait for security forces

☢️ Hazardous Materials

  • 1
    Isolate the area immediately – move people 50+ meters away
  • 2
    Do not touch / smell – identify from a distance if possible
  • 3
    Fire brigade: 118 with substance details if known | Toxin information: 145
  • 4
    Notify RSOC: +972 74-723-6000
  • 5
    Contact Site Safety Officers (Elsje / Catherine) and EHS
  • 6
    Evacuate as needed per rescue services instruction
