🔥 Fire / Smoke

  • 1
    Upon discovering fire – attempt to extinguish with the nearest fire extinguisher. If unable to control – step back and evacuate people around you.
  • 2
    Activate manual fire alarm button | Alert fire department: 102
  • 3
    Security Officer notifies RSOC: 074-723-6000/3/4
  • 4
    Evacuate employees via stairs – no elevators
  • 5
    ERT sweeps assigned floors, reports casualties/trapped persons to Security Officer
  • 6
    Assemble at assembly point | Conduct headcount | Report missing personnel
  • 7
    Prevent employees from re-entering – until clearance from rescue forces or authorized party

🚀 Rocket / Missile Alert

  • 1
    Upon receiving alert via Home Front Command app / hearing building siren – enter the nearest shelter (Mamad) immediately
  • 2
    If unable to reach shelter in time – go to the nearest stairwell and remain there
  • 3
    If outside the building – lie flat on the ground and cover your head with both hands
  • 4
    Remain in shelter until receiving an all-clear announcement from Home Front Command

🔫 Security Incident / Violence – Run · Hide · Fight

  • 1
    RUN – Evacuate the area immediately, leave belongings behind
  • 2
    HIDE – Lock/barricade door, turn off lights, stay low. If possible – enter the nearest shelter room.
  • 3
    FIGHT – As a last resort only, use any available means
  • 4
    Security Officer reports to Police 100 and RSOC 074-723-6000. Initiate lockdown.
  • 5
    Report to Police: 100
  • 6
    Do not attempt individual rescue – await security forces

🌍 Earthquake

  • 1
    Floors 1–2: Evacuate as quickly as possible via stairs out of the building
  • 2
    Direct employees into the nearest shelter room – leave the door open
  • 3
    Update RSOC: 074-723-6000/3/4
  • 4
    Once shaking stops – if possible, evacuate employees via stairs out of the building
  • 5
    Watch for aftershocks | Outdoors – keep people away from trees and hanging power lines
  • 6
    Do not authorize return to building without clearance from rescue forces or authorized party
  • 7
    Do not use elevators

🚑 Medical Emergency

  • 1
    Call ambulance (MDA): 101
  • 2
    AED – Main lobby near OM desk – retrieve immediately
  • 3
    Secure the area – prevent unnecessary people from entering
  • 4
    Security Officer notifies RSOC and ensures clear access for rescue teams
  • 5
    Do not move the casualty without proper training
  • 6
    Station someone at building entrance to direct MDA team

☢️ Hazardous Materials

  • 1
    Isolate the area immediately – keep employees at least 50 meters away
  • 2
    Do not touch or smell – identify from a distance if possible
  • 3
    Fire Department: 102 with substance details if known
  • 4
    Report to RSOC: 074-723-6000/3/4
  • 5
    Contact CBRE representative and EHS
  • 6
    Evacuate as needed per management decision
